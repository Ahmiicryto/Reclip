What is SnapLoad?

SnapLoad is a free, fast online video downloader supporting YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, Reddit and over 1000 other websites. No registration, no software install — paste a URL, click download, done.

How It Works

SnapLoad is powered by yt-dlp, the industry-leading open-source video extraction engine. Your URL is processed server-side — we automatically try multiple YouTube client strategies (android_vr, ios, mweb, web_embedded) to bypass bot detection without cookies in most cases.

YouTube Bot-Check Bypass

We try android_vr → android_embedded → android → ios → mweb → web_embedded clients in sequence. Most YouTube videos download successfully without any cookies. The cookie upload feature is a fallback for edge cases.
