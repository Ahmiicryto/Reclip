About SnapLoad

Free Video Downloader — Powered by yt-dlp

What is SnapLoad?

SnapLoad is a free, open-source video downloader that lets you save videos from YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, Reddit, and over 1000 other websites directly to your device.

How It Works

SnapLoad is powered by yt-dlp, the industry-leading open-source video extraction library. When you paste a URL, our server uses yt-dlp to fetch the video metadata and stream URLs, then serves the file directly to your browser. No middlemen, no data retention.

Privacy

We do not log URLs you paste, store downloaded videos beyond your session, or share any data with third parties. Downloaded files are automatically deleted after serving.

Supported Sites

Anything yt-dlp supports — over 1000 websites including YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Twitter/X, Facebook, Reddit, Vimeo, Twitch, Dailymotion, SoundCloud, Loom, Streamable, Pinterest, Tumblr, Threads, LinkedIn, and many more.
